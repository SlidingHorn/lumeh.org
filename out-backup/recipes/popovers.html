Popovers

Makes 6 popovers

Mix

  • 1.25 cups flour
  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp melted butter
  • 1/4 tsp salt

. Preheat oven to 450°F.

Spray the pan lightly with cooking spray. Place the pan in the oven for about two minutes to preheat.

Remove the pan from the oven and pour a bit of the

  • extra melted butter

into each cup. Distribute the batter evenly amongst the cups.

Bake at 450°F for 20 minutes and then at 325°F for 15 minutes.
