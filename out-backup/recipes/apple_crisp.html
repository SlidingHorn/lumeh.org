Apple Crisp

by Colin Chan

This is a half-recipe. Serves 4?

Apple

  • 1 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/4 c sugar
  • 1 Tbsp flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp cinnamon

Crisp

  • 3/4 c oats
  • 3/4 c flour
  • 1 c brown sugar
  • 3/4 tsp cinnamon
  • 3/4 c butter
  • 1/2 tsp salt

350°F
