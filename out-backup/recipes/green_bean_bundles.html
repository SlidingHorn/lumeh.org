Green Bean Bundles

by Ann Titus

Yields 24 bundles.

Ingredients

  • 4 cans whole green beans (not cut)
  • 1 lb bacon
  • 3/4 stick margarine
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • garlic salt
  • pepper

Procedure

Roll 16 green beans in 1/2 slice of bacon. Secure with a toothpick.

Melt butter. Stir in sugar, garlic salt, and pepper. Heat together until blended. Pour over bundles. Cover with foil.

Bake at 350°F for 45 minutes, then remove foil and bake 15 more minutes.
