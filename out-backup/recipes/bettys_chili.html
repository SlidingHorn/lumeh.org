Betty’s Chili

by Lisa Chan’s mom

Summary

2 Tbls Crisco brown brown add simmer for up to a day!
1 lb ground beef
1/2 cup onion chop
28 oz can tomatoes in juice
3 Tbls brown sugar
1 tsp chili powder
3/4 tsp salt
small can red kidney beans

Ingredients

  • 2 Tbls Crisco or vegetable oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 cup chopped onions
  • 28 oz can tomatoes in juice
  • 3 Tbls brown sugar
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • small can of red kidney beans, not drained
  • additional can of tomato puree or tomato juice or tomato soup (if needed to increase quantity)
